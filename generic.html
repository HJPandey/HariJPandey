Me, Myself, And I

Disclaimer! It is not about G-Eazy x Bebe Rexha music video.
I am merely trying to give you, my fellow readers, a little intro about me and what I think about myself.
And where I am @. Did I cover all - me,myself, and I?

I am a creative person, not necessarily a person that creates creative things. I think that I am a creative person because I like to create things and stay productive. I stay observant to the things that I find interesting, interesting enough that I learn something from them, critique them, and sugget a way in my head to make them even more interesting to me; Yeaah! I am judgemental, like, who isn't? I love giving myself a ton of work with deadlines; man, I love deadlines. Infact, I miss them. Professionally, I have interest in lot of things Architecture, Robotics, Skyscrapers (weird, because I am Acrophobic!), Structures, Automobile, Aerospace, Space,Project Management, Heating and Cooling. But If I have to choose, I would be an HVAC- Mechanical Design Engineer because it is an overlap of the sets of checklist that I made for my perfect Job's Venn diagram.

I am always in conflict with my interest in social media, its like Katy's Hot and Cold. That is why I am looking for a different insight on them, totally! Talking about where I am at and what I am doing, I am currently working as a Data Center Engineering Operations (DCEO) Tech. geek for Amazon Web Services, and it is really a cool gig for me and there is lot to learn and grow in this field and Amazon World. One thing about my work ethics; I know sometimes we slack off (trying to say I slack off too!, a lot!), my mindset about the work is that I worship my job because it pays your bill and stuff and it keeps your life going smoothly. Ain't that right?

There is lot to learn, lot to think, lot to plan, lot to do, and a lot to talk about. I will keep some more interesting stuff posted on my website if you would like to click/tap on my site. I give you my appreciation, my fellow readers, for taking your time to scroll the pages. Fancy a feedback on my page or even a word for me? Ping me! or lets socialize!
